About Me

Hello! I've been a marketing analytics professional for the last eight years, specializing in user behavior, audience/cohort analysis, and predictive modeling. Before that, I spent three awesome years teaching English in Yamanashi, Japan. The Bay Area is home, but as a San Diego native, I miss SoCal California burritos terribly. Ever had carne asada fries before?

I'm currently a Masters candidate at the University of San Francisco's Analytics program. I'm very interested in what makes people tick and how they behave. Specifically, I work on multi-channel attribution, instrumentation, machine learning, predictive modeling, and text (sentiment) analysis.
