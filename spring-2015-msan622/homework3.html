Functionality List

The following features were implemented in order to reach each corresponding grade level.

Basic Functionality
  • Generated multi-series line plot of the data
  • Animated time series bubble plot
Moderate Functionality
  • Panning: click and drag the selection within "context" to shift the larger detail view
  • Details-on-Demand: hovering shows tooltip details
Advanced Functionality
  • Brushing: highlight a selection from the "context" chart

Motivation

For the time series multi-line plot, I chose the template above because of it's multivariate possibilities. Multiple lines were drawn for different statistics, where shared units (here, different types of deaths) could leverage the same axis. This left the second axis to show another statistic (distance, in my case). I chose my colors using a six-class qualitative palette, just to mix it up from the typical red/blue/green.
